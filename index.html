Frequently Asked Questions

How much weight load can a bag chair support?

The weight load depends on the specific model of the sack chair, but they can usually support between 100 and 200 kg depending on the material and construction.

Can the sack chair be used outdoors?

Yes, there are special models of sack chairs for outdoor use, made of waterproof and weatherproof materials.

How do I care for a mech chair?

Most sack chairs have removable covers that can be easily removed and washed in a washing machine. It is also recommended to periodically fluff the filling.

How long does it take to deliver a sack chair?

Delivery time depends on the location and the shipping method chosen, it usually takes between one to three days.

Can the sack chair be used for children to play?

Yes, bag chairs are often used for playing and entertaining children due to their softness and comfort.

How do you choose the right size of a sack chair?

The size of the sack chair is chosen depending on preference and the place where it will be used. For home use, sizes from medium to large are usually suitable, and for children's rooms - compact models.
